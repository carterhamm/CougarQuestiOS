🐾 CougarQuest iOS

Welcome to CougarQuest iOS, the official mobile companion app for BYU Sports Camps! 📱 Designed specifically for campers, this sleek, easy-to-use app provides an interactive experience by engaging users in exciting challenges, competitions, and activities. Keep track of your quests, monitor your progress, and see where you rank among your peers, all from your iOS device!

🌟 Key Features:
 - 📋 Interactive Quests
Easily browse, join, and complete quests. Earn points and badges to showcase your achievements!
 - 🏆 Real-Time Leaderboards
Stay competitive by tracking your rankings against fellow campers in real-time.
 - 🛠️ Customizable Profiles
Personalize your camper profile, monitor your progress, and celebrate your accomplishments.
 - 🔑 Seamless Authentication
Smooth registration and sign-in process to get you started in seconds.
 - 🎉 Notifications & Reminders
Never miss a beat—stay updated with timely notifications about new quests and events.
 - 📸 Engaging Interface
Vibrant and intuitive UI ensures you have fun navigating and interacting with CougarQuest.

🛠️ Built With:
 - Swift & SwiftUI for seamless user experience and fast, modern interfaces.
 - Firebase for real-time backend functionality and secure data handling.
